Working with Liovinci

UI/UX Designer

Liovinci is a startup now available to IOs and Android that keeps users posted about the latest updates of all of their favorite projects and product! An interactive experience, users have the ability to track projects that peak their interest and comment on the most recent updates.

Working with Liovinci I’ve had a hand in the UI design of several of the website and app screens created through Adobe programs. Through Invision and Slack our mostly remote team is able to collaborate on the designs and test the updates to the app.

Although fairly new Liovinci is steadily increasing its amount of users. To download the app or just browse through our website please visit the link below!
