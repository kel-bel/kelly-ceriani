Blacksburg Museum and Cultural Foundation

Wordpress Designer

The Blacksburg Museum & Cultural Foundation was created to spearhead the restoration of the Alexander Black House with the vision of creating a place of community, gathering, and learning. A small local museum in the heart of the college town of Blacksburg, the museum and its staff became a sort of second home and creative workplace.

Working with the museum for a year I took on the project of creating a new updated web site for the museum and foundation. I used Wordpress themes and plugins to craft a personalized unique website that catered to the current and future needs of the museum.

Using both Photoshop and Illustrator I scoured through the museum’s albums and altered some of their excellent media for the website. The experience gave me a lot of practice using Wordpress and while at the museum I was also able to help created posters for the various events (an example is on Behance).
